About Us

Kasama is a bakery and modern Filipino restaurant from chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores located in the East Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago.

Michelin Star Award

Kasama became the world's first MICHELIN-star Filipino restaurant in 2022. We are so proud to have made a name for our great food and we are truly grateful for all of the support!
